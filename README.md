# Vortextech-datasci-week1
As we approach 2030, people might ask how would AI affect people's work or is AI really going to replace many jobs.
Ofcourse there isn't an accurate answer to this question, but some studies and datasets try to clear and create an image of what could happen. 

I created a bar chart, histogram and scatter plot to visualize the data. And i observed that there isn't a strong correlation between AI risks and future demand of jobs. beacause the scatter plot was responsible for the visualization of the AI risks and Future demand we saw that there is a weak correlation between AI risks and Future demand. which means that a job's risk of being replaced by AI does not influence its predicted future demand. 

the histogram showed the average salaries of the roles or jobs stated in the dataset, and the bar chart stated the hiring trends across roles in 2026 and we concluded that ,based on the records, hiring trends are stable or growing while declining hiring trends are slightly less frequent.
