As we approach 2030, people might ask how would AI affect people's work ? or is AI really going to replace many jobs?
Of course there isn't an accurate answer to this question, but some studies and datasets try to clear and create an image of what could happen. I chose a huge 3000 row 20 column dataset from kaggle in order to analyze & predict what could happen to the job market as we approach 2030. here is what i created....

I created a bar chart, histogram and scatter plot to visualize the data using matplotlib, seaborn, NumPy, and Pandas. I observed that there isn't a strong correlation between AI risks and future demand of jobs. Because the scatter plot visualized the relationship between AI risks and future demand, we observed that there is a weak correlation between AI risks and Future demand. Which means that a job's risk of being replaced by AI does not influence its predicted future demand. 

The histogram showed the average salaries of the roles or jobs stated in the dataset, and the bar chart stated the hiring trends across roles in 2026. We concluded that ,based on the records, hiring trends are stable or growing while declining hiring trends are slightly less frequent.
